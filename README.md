Department Management System (DMS) is a web application designed to reduce paperwork and help manage academic operations digitally for a University department. Typically, Students have to fill out numerous UG forms, such as Transcript Request, FYP Proposals, Leave applications, on paper, which often get misplaced, take a lot of time for approvals, and aren't transparent. Thus, DMS allows Students, Teachers, and Administrators to have their own role-based portal for performing required operations. The students are allowed to sign up, log in to their accounts, update their profiles, and upload UG forms online. Teachers can keep track of students' activities and Admin has full authorization. Teachers can access their profile, and student-related activities, and Admins have complete control over every action. The tech stack used is React.js and Tailwind CSS for the frontend and Node.js, Express.js, and MongoDB for the backend. As it is developed as a web application, DMS is platform-independent and mobile-responsive. Being a web app, it can be deployed for multiple departments and even multiple institutions. On testing, DMS obtained successful testing without errors, covering all the test cases, which concludes our effort towards providing a hassle-free solution to department paperwork.
